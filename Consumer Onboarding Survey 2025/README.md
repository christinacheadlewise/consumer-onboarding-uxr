# Consumer Onboarding Drop-off Survey 2025

- **Question:** Why don't new consumers convert within 30 days?
- **Data collected:** 22 Oct – 25 Nov 2025
- **Sample:** 1,331 cleaned responses (US, UK, Philippines, English only)
- **Enrichment:** Matched to Snowflake LTV + onboarding flow data
- **Lead:** Peter Yeomans
- **Analysis:** Christina Cheadle

## Key files

- [Site with full findings](../docs/index.md)
- [Claude skill for team Q&A](dropoff-survey-insights.md) (also in `.claude/skills/`)

## Top-level findings

1. Most "drop-offs" already used Wise for a different product before the survey
2. Trust hesitators are the highest-value customers (2x LTV)
3. Signup friction depresses long-term value more than it blocks adoption
4. 21% of send/receive users don't know about the card — biggest actionable lever
5. Invest/Assets has a 47% drop-off rate (product gap, not onboarding)
6. Speed perception affects long-term value
7. Recent movers (<6 months) are highest-value new customers
