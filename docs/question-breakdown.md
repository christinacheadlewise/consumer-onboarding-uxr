# Every Question, With Revenue Data

Full response distributions for all survey questions. "Med Rev" is median lifetime revenue in GBP for customers who chose that answer. "Med Vol" is median lifetime transaction volume in GBP.

Data collected: 22 October – 25 November 2025.

---

## Q1: How old are you?
*Everyone answered (n=1,307)*

| Answer | n | % | Med Rev | Med Vol |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Under 18 | 1 | 0.1% | — | — |
| 18-24 | 231 | 17.7% | £24 | £6,295 |
| 25-34 | 289 | 22.1% | £28 | £6,700 |
| 35-44 | 201 | 15.4% | £23 | £5,946 |
| 45-54 | 220 | 16.8% | £28 | £6,996 |
| 55-64 | 204 | 15.6% | £27 | £6,288 |
| 65+ | 161 | 12.3% | £26 | £7,599 |

No meaningful LTV difference by age. Well-distributed sample.

## Q4: Describe your signup experience
*Everyone answered (n=1,331)*

| Answer | n | % | Med Rev |
|---|---|---|---|
| Extremely easy | 773 | 58% | £28 |
| Somewhat easy | 366 | 28% | £25 |
| Neither | 124 | 9% | £17 |
| Somewhat difficult | 63 | 5% | £19 |
| Extremely difficult | 5 | 0.4% | £31 |

86% found it easy. Easier signup = higher revenue (p=0.0002). The 14% who found it difficult/neutral have meaningfully lower LTV.
